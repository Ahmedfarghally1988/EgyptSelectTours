To secure your reservation:

To secure your reservation, you must inform Egypt Select Tours of your request. A company representative will contact you to explain and finalize your request and then send the itinerary for final confirmation. You must confirm all details and pay the entry fee to the Egypt Select Tours agency by transfer or credit/debit card (Visa or MasterCard).

Changes made by the traveler:

Any changes made to the original booking will be subject to availability. Administrative fees will not apply except for airline tickets if the full payment has already been completed and the voucher is issued.

Payment and Booking Policy:

We offer an entry fee of just 20% to guarantee your reservation, including hotel, cabin, domestic flights, and other relevant services. The rest of the amount can be paid up to two days before you arrive at the final destination or in cash upon arrival at your leading tour. Your reservation will be a risk-free investment.

Payment via credit card:

For payments via credit card, according to the regulations of our bank, a document is required to allow the deduction of the amount on your credit card by our finance department, see:

A signed authorization letter to Egypt Select Tours, allowing you to withdraw the required amount from your credit card. The signature must match what appears on your credit card and passport.

This document must be sent by email to Egypt Select Tours or delivered to our representative upon arrival at the airport. Otherwise, we will not be able to proceed with your request.

Cancellation Policy (Not included for high season):

Cancellation requests must be sent by fax or email to provide the company with written confirmation that your reservation must be canceled.

If you decide to cancel your trip, the following rate table will apply:

  • From the booking date up to 61 days before your arrival, 10% of the total tour price will be charged.
  • Between 60 and 31 days before your arrival date, 25% of the tour price will be charged.
  • Between 30 and 15 days before your arrival date, 50% of the tour price will be charged.
  • Between 14 and 1 day before your arrival date, 100% of the total tour price will be charged.

Circumstances of force majeure:

Egypt Select Tours offers a flexible range of cancellation policies in case the government or airline issues any notice from the country of departure or final destination. If this happens, we will refund the amount paid for the tour package, or, in some cases, your payment will be available as a voucher that can be used in any of the locations where we operate. You can use the coupon on any reservation made within 12 months, and reservations will be adjusted to reflect these travel credits automatically. According to the airline’s cancellation policy, only flight cancellation fees will be charged, and bank surcharges will be applied to the deposit amount paid.
